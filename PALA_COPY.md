## How It Works

**Step 1: Film Your Game**
Record your match or practice session on your phone—Pala works with any footage.

**Step 2: Upload to Pala**
Drop your video into the app. Pala handles the rest.

**Step 3: Get Your Breakdown**
Pala analyzes your game and serves up a clear performance summary with key insights.

**Step 4: Watch the Walkthrough**
Pala guides you through your best and trickiest plays with voiceover commentary.

**Step 5: Ask Questions**
Pause at any moment and ask Pala anything about that play—get answers in real time.
